Sudden Cardiac Arrest Can Happen Anywhere

AEDCare empowers communities with education and awareness so bystanders can save lives before emergency responders arrive.

Why AEDs Matter

Sudden cardiac arrest is one of the leading causes of death. Survival decreases rapidly without early defibrillation.

  • Survival drops 7–10% per minute without AED use
  • Most cardiac arrests occur outside hospitals
  • Early AED use can double or triple survival rates

Our Mission

AEDCare exists to make life-saving knowledge common knowledge. Through education and preparedness, we reduce fear and increase action when seconds matter.

Community Education

AEDs are designed to be used by anyone. We focus on practical, real-world education so ordinary people feel confident responding to cardiac emergencies.
