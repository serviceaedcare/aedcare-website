Sudden Cardiac Arrest Can Happen Anywhere

AEDCare educates communities so ordinary people can save lives before emergency responders arrive.

Why AEDs Matter

Sudden cardiac arrest is one of the leading causes of death. Survival decreases rapidly without early defibrillation.

  • Survival drops 7–10% per minute without AED use
  • Most cardiac arrests occur outside hospitals
  • Early AED use can double or triple survival

Our Mission

AEDCare exists to make life-saving knowledge common knowledge. Through education, awareness, and preparedness, we reduce fear and increase action when seconds matter.

Community Education

We focus on real-world understanding of AEDs so bystanders feel confident using them. AEDs are designed to be used by anyone — training removes hesitation.
